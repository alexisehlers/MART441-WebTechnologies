The Adventures of Flutterby the Butterfly



Flutterby the butterfly had wandered long and far, but she realized something was missing. She didn't know what the word meant, but her friend the Ladybug told her she was lonely.
One day, Flutterby felt the urge to fly far far away. She looked right and left, but wasnt sure where to go.
Flutterby finally decided she had two choices. North or South.
